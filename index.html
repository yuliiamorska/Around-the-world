Eating out in Copenhagen can be rather expensive. Look for restaurants outside the city center, or follow one of the many 'buffet' signs (which come at around 100-200 Kroner). The Copenhagen Card offers discounts and free public transport. You can purchase it online, at your hotel or 7-Eleven. Check the website for a full list of sales points. Although tipping is said to be included in salaries, keep in mind that Copenhagen is expensive, and servers rely on tips.

Six Places to Rent a Bicycle in Berlin

Yes, Berlin does in fact have its own island filled with museums! Situated on this small island on the river Spree sits five world-renowned museums: the Pergamonmuseum, the Bode-Museum, the Neus Museum, the Alte Nationalgalerie and the Altes Museum. They were nearly destroyed during the war but have been beautifully restored and the island is now a UNESCO World Heritage site.
